Hello, I'm Nastaran

I am a PhD student interested in conducting research on sociotechnical systems, with a particular focus on how technology, institutions, and human values interact in high-stakes contexts. My work examines how affected communities—especially patient and advocacy groups—engage with complex technical and policy infrastructures, and how these systems can be designed to better support participation, accountability, and collective decision-making. Through qualitative and computational approaches, I aim to understand and reimagine how expertise, lived experience, and power are negotiated in contemporary digital systems.
